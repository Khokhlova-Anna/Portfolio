My path to UX/UI Design :

1. I’ve started my career with an internship in UX designer/ Front-End developer position.
It helped me to make my firs steps in UX and also to understand the developers’ point of view better.

2. My next goal was to gain the knowledge of the company’s internal kitchen and different projects’ flows and achieve the confidence in the organization of the whole process from UX conception and UI deliverables, throughout the development phase and till the testing and quality assurance of the final product.

Now, having almost 4 years of experience in Project Management in third-party applications maintenance and consulting these are my skills and achievements I am leaving this position with :

- Leading the team on my projects I am used to facing the problems, which helped me become a solution-oriented person.

- Working on multiple projects at the same time I not only developed my multitasking skills and the ability to switch between completely different subjects but it also taught me the importance of prioritizing and integrating well-though processes to every aspect of everyday work.

- Being in touch with multiple players on the project field such as UX/UI team, software engineers, quality assurance engineers, SEO team, Sales department and coordinating them helped me to better understand the corporate structure from the inside and gain the stakeholders management skill.

- Having most of the team members in different countries I have adapted myself to remote work long before it became a necessity.

- Building a solid relationship with clients helped me to develop empathy and understanding of people’s needs as well as business challenges.

3. Third step was to deepen my UX/UI knowledge and improve my design skills. Which is why I’ve taken two courses :
- UX professional course in UX Design Institute
and
- UI design for UX designers by DesignLab

4. Then I’ve started working as a freelancer and made two projects.

All these steps made me more confident and finally I am ready to seek for a UX/UI position.
